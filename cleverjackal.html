The Blue Jackal - Worksheet

A clever jackal once accidentally fell into a drum of blue dye. When he emerged, he was entirely blue! When he reached the jungle, the other animals, amazed by his unusual appearance and a little afraid of him, made him king. The jackal enjoyed pretending to be the king, he loved the power and respect that came with the role. However, the truth was eventually revealed when the jackal forgot himself and started calling in his real voice, revealing himself to be a fraud.

The animals chased him out of the forest and the blue jackal's lies were exposed.

4. What is the story about?
