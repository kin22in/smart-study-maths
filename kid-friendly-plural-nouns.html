Fun with Plural Nouns!

Click on each card to see examples of different plural noun rules!

REGULAR NOUNS

Add -S

car→cars
cat→cats
house→houses
rat→rats
toy→toys
ENDS IN CH, SH, S, X or Z

Add -ES

blitz→blitzes
box→boxes
bus→buses
bush→bushes
watch→watches
ENDS IN F or FE

Remove F/FE, Add -VES

knife→knives
life→lives
shelf→shelves
wife→wives
wolf→wolves
ENDS IN VOWEL + O

Add -S

embryo→embryos
radio→radios
stereo→stereos
video→videos
zoo→zoos
ENDS IN CONSONANT + O

Add -ES

hero→heroes
potato→potatoes
tomato→tomatoes
photo→photos (exception)
piano→pianos (exception)
IRREGULAR NOUNS

Special Changes

child→children
foot→feet
goose→geese
man→men
mouse→mice
ENDS IN VOWEL + Y

Add -S

day→days
boy→boys
key→keys
toy→toys
guy→guys
ENDS IN CONSONANT + Y

Remove Y, Add -IES

baby→babies
city→cities
fly→flies
lady→ladies
story→stories
NO CHANGE

Same in Singular & Plural

fish→fish
fruit→fruit
sheep→sheep
species→species
swine→swine
