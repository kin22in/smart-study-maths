The Rescue of Max - Worksheet

Sophie was walking home from school on a chilly autumn afternoon when she heard a faint whimpering from behind a pile of leaves. Curiosity piqued, she pushed aside the leaves and found a small, shivering dog with a dirty coat. The dog looked up at her with big, sad eyes. Sophie immediately knew she had to help. The dog had no collar or tags, so Sophie decided to call him Max. She gently picked him up and wrapped him in her warm scarf. Max was scared, but he slowly began to relax in Sophie's arms. Sophie knew she couldn't just leave him there, so she took him to her house.

At home, Sophie's parents were surprised to see her with a dog. They quickly agreed to help Max. Sophie gave him a warm bath and a hearty meal. Max's coat began to shine, and his tail wagged with happiness. Sophie's parents made some calls and found that no one had reported a missing dog matching Max's description. The next day, Sophie's family took Max to the vet for a check-up. The vet confirmed that Max was healthy but had been neglected. Sophie felt a surge of happiness knowing she had made a difference. Over time, Max became a beloved member of the family. Sophie learned that even small acts of kindness could change a life, and Max's grateful eyes were a daily reminder of her brave rescue.
