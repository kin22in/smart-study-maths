The Little Red Bird

Once there was a little red bird named Ruby. Ruby lived in a tall tree in the forest. She loved to sing beautiful songs every morning.

One day, Ruby woke up feeling sad. She had lost her favorite shiny pebble that she kept in her nest.

"Where could my pebble be?" Ruby asked her friends. The squirrel hadn't seen it. The rabbit hadn't seen it. No one knew where it was.

Ruby flew down to the ground to look for her pebble. She searched near the big rock. She looked under the leaves. She checked by the small stream.

When Ruby got to the fox's den, she saw something shiny. It was her pebble! But the fox was sleeping right next to it.

Ruby had an idea. She sang her most beautiful song. The fox woke up and smiled at the lovely music.

"Hello, Fox," said Ruby. "I think that's my pebble next to you. May I have it back, please?"

The fox looked at the pebble. "Is this yours? I found it by the stream. I thought it was pretty, so I brought it home."

"Yes, it's my special pebble. I use it to decorate my nest," explained Ruby.

"You can have it back," said the fox kindly. "Thank you for your beautiful song."

Ruby took her pebble and flew back to her nest. She was so happy to have her treasure again! From that day on, Ruby and the fox became good friends. Ruby would sing for the fox, and sometimes the fox would find pretty things to share with Ruby.
